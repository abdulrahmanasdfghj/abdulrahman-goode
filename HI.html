🌍 My Favorite Travel Destinations

🇶🇦 Qatar

Qatar is a blend of modern skylines, desert beauty, and cultural treasures.

Top Highlights:

  • Doha Corniche
  • Souq Waqif
  • Museum of Islamic Art
  • Inland Sea (Khor Al Adaid)

Food to Try:

  • Machboos
  • Luqaimat
  • Karak tea
  • Choice snack: Shawarma 🌯 or Falafel 🧆

🇪🇬 Egypt

Egypt is timeless, where ancient wonders meet the vibrant Nile.

Top Highlights:

  • Pyramids of Giza & the Sphinx
  • Egyptian Museum
  • Luxor & Karnak Temples
  • Red Sea

Food to Try:

  • Koshari
  • Ful & Ta‘meya
  • Konafa & Basbousa
  • Choice snack: Shawarma 🌯 or Falafel 🧆

✨ Travel Tips

  • Dress modestly in cultural sites.
  • Stay hydrated and carry sunscreen.
  • Keep cash for markets.
  • Agree on taxi fares or use apps.
